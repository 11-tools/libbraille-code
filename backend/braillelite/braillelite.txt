Braille Lite Driver for Libbraille
----------------------------------

This driver was developped thanks to a terminal lended by Sylvie
Duchateau and has been tested on a BrailleLite 40.

Keys to put in PC Mode

space + 345 then space + p (or 1234 )


Serial configuration
--------------------

(config braillelite sylvie : 9600, no parity, semi duplex, 8 bits, 1
stop bit, xon/xoff flow control, CR+LF activated)
